The Place

A view that rewrites the evening.

Six floors above the old centre, the city unfolds beneath you: the Palace lit gold, rooftops fading into the hills, the river catching the last of the light. From golden hour to two in the morning, the room changes with the sky.

01 � Spirit

The soul of the drink.

Mezcal, house aged six months in our rooftop cellar. Smoke, earth, a trace of something floral. We pour it generously, because restraint is not the Mirador way.

02 � Shake

Twelve seconds of certainty.

The shake is performance, a contract between bartender and guest. Twelve hard seconds, no second guessing. You hear the ice change pitch the moment it is ready.

03 � Pour

From cloud to crystal.

Double strained into a chilled coupe in a single high arc. The foam settles, the colour deepens, and what was chaos in the tin becomes clarity in the glass.

04 � Serve

Yours, entirely.

A citrus twist, a nod, a small bow. The first sip is yours to find. We have already given it ours. Welcome to Mirador.
